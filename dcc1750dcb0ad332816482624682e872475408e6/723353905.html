Eastman.org Homepage

Primary Navigation Menu 

The primary navigation menu is found at the top of every page on Eastman.org.  We propose seven (7) top-level links including the following: About, Visit, Collections, Exhibitions, Access, Learn, Join & Give.  We will also utilize a user-centric secondary navigation to provide additional functionality with very specific calls to action, including a global search feature.  All top-level navigation elements will also utilize content prepared on a landing page to provide ease-of-use along with a more accessible user experience.  All navigation elements will conform to WCAG 2.0 AA web accessibility guidelines.

About

This section will consist of all information related to the mission, vision, values of the George Eastman Museum.  Links to annual report landing pages, employment landing pages, and the generic contact form will reside here as well.

Adding an FAQ section here could improve the experience for new "visitors".

Visit

The visit section will contain information related to an offline visit to the Eastman Museum.

The "Plan Your Visit" sub-navigation has been improved through consolidation with a "What to do" sub-nav grouping. We also introduced a "Policies" sub-nav to organize any policies/procedures/rules.


Room for Improvement

We should attempt to improve upon the "What to Do" and "Policies" navigation during the build process.
