Welcome to the George Eastman Museum Website confluence space!

The George Eastman Museum (GEM) is located in Rochester, New York, on the estate of George Eastman, the pioneer of popular photography and motion picture film. Founded in 1947 as an independent nonprofit institution, it is the world’s oldest photography museum and one of the oldest film archives.

The current website was launched in 2015 but was developed in a piecemeal fashion lending to technical debt, module conflicts and administrative pain points. The recent Drupalgeddon zero-day exploits have exponentially increased the impact of the initial build’s failures, further mandating an upgrade to Drupal 8. This upgrade must leverage Drupal and industry best practices to mitigate risk, minimize technical debt, and facilitate a sustainable long-term implementation.

GEM has approached HRS Ventures, Inc. (DropForge Labs) to architect, design and develop an enhanced Drupal 8 implementation consisting of a streamlined user experience, easy administration of data/content, the ability to quickly disseminate important information, mobile compatibility, and increased operational efficiency. Due to the nature of GEM’s service offerings, organizational structure, operations within the non-profit sector, and resource availability, DropForge Labs understands the mutual importance of establishing a long-term relationship with GEM. Through this relationship, DropForge Labs can provide a variety of services including a consultative and agile approach to project planning, design and development services, training, website hosting, website maintenance and website support. DropForge Labs’ software expertise and catalog of existing work will provide for a reliable solution that meets the business requirements of stakeholders, whilst accommodating future growth to further expand upon the vision of both DropForge Labs and GEM.

This project represents an opportunity to improve upon the existing Eastman.org website through the strategic development of a Drupal 8 powered solution.

By leveraging our expertise and industry best practices, the proposed solution will achieve the following deliverable derived goals:

  • Development of an easy to administer web application, designed, architected and built by DropForge
  • Creation of a fully responsive, cross-device capable application that is reliable and accessible Enablement of staff to update and maintain content site-wide
  • Streamlined operational efficiency through the following features:
    • A customized Drupal 8 authoring experience enabling UI-driven layout changes o Power User capabilities to enable editing of inline HTML, JS and CSS
    • Integration with Google analytics
    • Metadata integration including Facebook OG tags and tracking codes
    • The use of Apache SOLR search technology for faceted search and improved “related content”
    • Enhanced media browser capabilities - including cropping, alt text etc.
  • Adoption of WCAG 2.0 LEVEL AA accessibility standards, whilst maintaining the existing “look and feel” Migration of all existing content to the new Drupal 8 website
  • Expedient collaboration with the Eastman team to foster improvements and deliver within a short timeframe
